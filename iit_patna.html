ABOUT COLLEGE

The first Prime minister of India Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru initiated the process of starting Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) in 1953 with the objective of rapidly progressing in the field of Science and Technology through collaboration with world class institutions. IITs have two prime objectives: (a) to perform high quality teaching and research, and (b) to produce high quality teachers and researchers.

Indian Institute of Technology Patna (abbreviated IIT Patna or IITP) is a public technical university located in Patna, India.[2] It is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India.[3] It is one of the new IITs established by an Act of the Indian Parliament on August 6, 2008.[4]

IIT Patna's campus is located at Amhara, Bihta, 35 km from Patna at a 501 acres (203 ha) site.[1][6][7][8] The foundation stone of the IIT Patna, Bihta Campus was laid by Kapil Sibal in 2011.[9] IIT Patna started its new session (from July 2015) in its permanent campus located at Bihta. The campus at Bihta was inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on 25 July 2015.

Placements

The institute has released its 2021 placement report and a total of 125 companies participated this year. A total of 175 students got placed among which 140 students were from B.Tech & 35 students were from M.Tech. Around 223 Placement offers were made to the students. The highest CTC of INR 54.57 LPA was offered to the B.Tech students & INR 52.50 LPA was offered to the M.Tech students. The average CTC stood at INR 16.17 LPA for the B.Tech students & INR 12.11 LPA for the M.Tech students & INR 6.97 LPA was offered to the M.Sc students. The institute is also conducting its summer internship placements for the 2021 batch and till now, 83 students have been offered internship
